Movie Recommender

A small AI project that ranks suggestions and explains its limits.

PythonRecommendation logicEvaluationModel limits

Overview

The movie recommender was an AI project where I had to make the word “recommendation” concrete. The system ranked movie suggestions based on user preference information and item features, then I evaluated whether those rankings made sense.

Problem

AI can sound more impressive than it is when the assumptions are hidden. The real problem was deciding what data mattered, how to score suggestions, and how to judge whether the result was useful.

Process

This is the basic path I followed while developing or analyzing the artifact.

01Defined the input features and scoring approach.
02Built the ranking logic and compared generated suggestions.
03Looked at cases where the model performed well or poorly.
04Wrote about the limits instead of pretending the model was smarter than it was.

Value

This artifact shows original thinking inside constraints. It also lets me talk about AI honestly: useful, interesting, and limited.

Reflection

The project taught me that explaining a model is almost as important as building it.
